Technovision 2017

( A National Level Technical Paper Presentation Event )

Organised By

Government College Of Engineering, Jalgaon

Date :

12 Octomber, 2017

Rules and Regulations

  1. Diploma, U.G. and P.G. students can participate in the event.
  2. Maximum 3 authors per paper can participate in the event.
  3. Theme of the paper shall be technology based and it is not limited to themes mentioned on the poster.
  4. Paper should not exceed 08 pages. Paper must be in IEEE format. (Paper should be essentially in a two column format; Font Style: Times New Roman; Font Size: 12; Headings: Bold and font size 14; Spacing: 1.5 )
  5. Last date for submission of Abstract/full length paper on the registered E-mail id is 6th October,2017. Mention your contact details along with it.
  6. Students are requested to send their paper on the following email-id : contact.technovision@gcoej.ac.in
  7. Processing fees at the time of abstract submission is Rs.50 (non-refundable) and after the paper has been selected, corresponding team has to pay Rs.300. Total fees per paper is Rs.350.
  8. Inauguration will start at 9 a.m. Every participant is expected to reach the venue before the inaugural function. Bring your college I-Card on event day compulsory.
  9. Each team will get 7 minutes for presentation followed by 3 minutes of question and answer session.
  10. Soft-copies of the submitted paper and PowerPoint presentations must be of MS office 2007-2013.
  11. Certificates shall be given to only those candidates who will present the paper.
  12. For any circumstances, final decision of the organizers and judges will be binding to all.
  13. Every participant has to strictly abide by the rules and regulations made by the institute and any violation of the rules may result in rejection of the paper or cancellation of the registration.

  1. Pay Processing fees of Rs. 50/- (Compulsory, Non-Refundable) for Abstract submission on given payment details below.
  2. Send your Abstract/Full length Paper and Scanned copy of Receipt along with following details on the E-mail contact.technovision@gcoej.ac.in

  5. Fees can be paid online at the details given below​

  6. Important dates
    • Full length paper submission : 6th October,2017
    • Acceptance, revision & Notification : 09th October,2017
    • Display of Presentation schedule : 10th October,2017
    • Paper submission Email : contact.technovision@gcoej.ac.in

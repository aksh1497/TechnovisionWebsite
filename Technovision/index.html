About Technovision

TECHNOVISION is a national level paper presentation competition conducted by Govt. College Of Engineering, Jalgaon. Conceptualized in 2015, Technovision provides a platform to students pursuing engineering studies to showcase their technical research and presentation skills on a national stage.

About GCOEJ

Government College Of Engineering, Jalgaon is an autonomous institute of Government of Maharashtra. It is one of the well-known college of the region. It is the only autonomous institute of engineering in the North Maharashtra University
Vision : "Globally Accepted Engineers with Human Skills"

Rules and Regulations

  1. Eligibility of participation and submitting paper shall be from Engg. stream diploma, UG & PG of all branches.
  2. Maximum 3 authors are allowed per Paper.
  3. Theme of the paper shall be technology based and it is not limited to themes mentioned on the poster.
  4. Abstract should not exceed one page and complete paper should not exceed 10 pages.
    Paper should be in IEEE format.
    • Two column format
    • Font style: Times New Roman
    • Font Size: 12
    • Heading: Bold & font size: 14
    • Spacing: 1.5
  5. Paper must be preceded by a cover page specifying the title of the paper, names of authors, their college names and their contact numbers with email id. Cover Page Template
  6. Paper submitted should be strictly in PDF Format. Please bring your Powerpoint presentation in a pen drive.
  7. Papers without payment of processing fees will not be considered and entertained.
  8. Last Date of submission of ABSTRACT of paper to be submit along with processing fees receipt scan/photo on registered email is till 3:00 PM on 06th OCTOBER 2017. (Extended Upto 7th October 2017 till 14:00 IST )
  9. Email for submission : contact.technovision@gcoej.ac.in
  10. The mail should contain :
    • Subject of Email : " ABSTRACT/PAPER FOR TECHNOVISION 2K17 "
    • SCAN/PHOTO COPY OF PROCESSING FEES RECEIPT.
    • Title : Theme of paper
    • Name(s) of author(s).
    • Phone number of all author(s)
    • College Name
  11. Final list of shortlisted papers will be displayed on website by 09 October 2017
  12. Participant required to produce college I-Card & processing fee receipt at the time of event.
  13. All teams and their respective members should report the registration desk before 8.00 am on day of event. Submit 3 hard copies of the shortlisted paper on the day of the event at registration desk.
  14. The teams will get 8 minutes for presentation and 2 minutes for conclusion followed by a Q&A session.

About

Abhivyakti, one of the cultural fest of Government College Of Engineering Jalgaon,is a moments in the form of 13 cultural events in and around the humongous campus to explore their unexplored interests.


OUR VISION

Abhivyakti 2017 has one vision, to make each enliven the ‘wish-to-be true’ through the world of numerous cultural events and strengthening our community through social life.


PROGRAM DETAILS

Events : Will be held on 13th Oct, 2016

Inaugural : On 12th Oct, 2017 from 09:00 am to 10:30 am

Lunch : At Mess at 01:00 pm onwards.
